About Our Therapist

Anthony has multiple years experience counseling individuals from all walks of life. He specializes in treating depression, anxiety, relationship issues and issues revolving around self-worth. He has clinical experience working in inpatient hospitals, community mental health and community corrections settings. His approach is to meet client's where they are at and walk alongside them as they work on their journey of self-discovery towards a more fullfilling life.
